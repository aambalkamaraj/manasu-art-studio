Our Philosophy

Preserving Ancient Heritage through Meticulous Craft

At Manasu Art Studio, we are committed to reviving and celebrating traditional Indian art forms. Operating from our boutique space in Singapore, we specialize in the historic style of Thanjavur (Tanjore) paintings, which dates back to the Chola empire. Every panel is created on a seasoned wooden board, layered with muck relief work, adorned with 22-carat gold leaf, and set with Jaipur semi-precious gemstones.

In addition to our gold-leaf sacred iconography, we bridge classical styles with contemporary art through hyper-realistic charcoal and graphite portrait sketching. We translate family photographs and animal portraits into fine art keepsakes with meticulous shading and contrast.

Whether you want to place a custom painting on your home altar, commission a realistic portrait of a loved one, or learn the art yourself in our weekend workshops, we bring authentic craftsmanship straight to Singapore.
